(“Mama always said life was like a box of chocolates. You never know what you're gonna get.”)

The basic idea is to create a web app that gathers, through a series of questions, various information from the users
(ex: “How much free time do you have in a day?”, “What’s your favorite hobby?”, “Who is your favorite actor?”, etc.)
So, it is possible to recommend a series of movies, even belonging to different genres, which follow the user’s preferences.
